Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


Q. How often should I have my HVAC system serviced?

A. The HVAC system is very complex and made up of multiple parts. An experienced HVAC contractor should do the system maintenance every six months to ensure that it is operating properly. The HVAC maintenance must be performed once in the fall season before the time system will be used the most.

Q. What maintenance does an air conditioner need?

A. A professional HVAC service provider must do air conditioner maintenance. Mainly, the following things should be done at the time of the maintenance. Air filters must be cleaned or replaced if necessary. Cleaning of the coils of the air conditioner. Coil fins must be straightened.
