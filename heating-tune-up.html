Heating tune-up

  • Check and adjust thermostat
  • Check all safeties and fan limit control
  • Check temperature rise
  • Check all controls
  • Clean and adjust burners
  • Clean and adjust pilot
  • Check heat exchanger for cracks

  • Check complete furnace cycle
